About Forest Finds

Forest Finds co. was established in 2008 by Roberto Frizzi and Emily Miller. Roberto Frizzi is an Italian immigrant who moved to Canada in 1990, where he met Emily Miller. Roberto has been working in the hospitality industry for over 15 years. In 1998, after having a child, tired of the hectic city life, they decided to raise her in the woods. They supported their family tree planting and mushroom picking. In 2005, they moved back to Vancouver, but their passion for the Canadian forest was still alive and burning. They then used their savings to purchase a food truck, and founded Forest Finds to bring their love for the woods into the city. In 2012 they were able to open their first restaurant.

The Forager's way

It is a well known fact that we should be kind to nature. By foraging, we accept everything the nature offers us and work our menus around that. This is why our menu changes so often, depending on the season and on the food's availability. Foraging is also an excellent way to create jobs for our community, and increases our awareness in regards to the ecosystem sorrounding us. What is there not to like?
